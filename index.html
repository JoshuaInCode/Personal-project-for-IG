About Me

Welcome to My World

I am a passionate speaker specializing in the fascinating intersection of neuroscience and technology. My presentations explore how understanding the human brain can revolutionize our approach to artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, and cognitive enhancement.

With a deep background in both neuroscience research and technology innovation, I bring a unique perspective that bridges scientific rigor with practical applications. My work focuses on how brain science can inform better technology design and how technology can enhance human cognitive capabilities.

When I'm not speaking at events, you can find me conducting research, mentoring emerging professionals in neurotechnology, and exploring the elegant complexity of the human mind through cutting-edge scientific methods.
